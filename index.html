About the Artist

My Artistic Journey

Art has been my way of seeing the world since I was a child. This early fascination naturally evolved into a deep passion for digital visual arts, especially illustration. Today, I use the digital canvas as my primary medium to explore and translate the emotions and experiences that shape my perspective.

Primarily working digitally helps me articulate emotions and experiences with illustrative detail. Yet, I also find joy and a different kind of expressive release in abstract oil painting on canvas. These varied approaches allow me to tell different facets of my story through art.
